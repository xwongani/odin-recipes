Sweet manhattan cocktail

Description

If you're a whiskey lover, you'll love this sweet manhattan, made with whiskey, vermouth and bitters. Serve with a maraschino cherry and a twist of lemon

Ingredients

  • 50ml bourbon rye whiskey
  • 25ml rosso vermouth
  • 5ml syrup from a jar of maraschino cherries (we used Luxardo)
  • 2 dashes bitters
  • ice for the garnish
  • maraschino cherry
  • a twist of pared lemon

Steps

  1. Stir the ingredients with ice in a mixing glass, then strain into a cocktail glass. Garnish and serve.
