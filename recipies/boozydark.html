Boozy dark delight

Description

Enjoy this night-cap cocktail to round off your evening. Made with stout, whisky and orange liqueur, it's smooth and rich â€“ ideal for cooler evenings

Ingredients

  • small handful of ice cubes (optional)
  • 25ml orange liqueur
  • 25ml whisky
  • 200ml stout
  • chocolate orange shavings to garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Drop a few ice cubes, if using, into a short glass and pour over the orange liqueur and whisky.

  2. Top up the drink with the stout, then garnish with the chocolate orange shavings, if you like.
